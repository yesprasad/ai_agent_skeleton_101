WELLNESS BENEFITS HANDBOOK

1. GYM REIMBURSEMENT
Employees enrolled in the PPO or HMO plan are eligible for up to $300 per year in gym membership reimbursements. Submit proof of payment and gym membership certificate.

2. SMOKING CESSATION PROGRAM
This benefit includes coverage for nicotine patches, gum, and up to 6 counseling sessions per year. Program is available to all full-time employees.

3. PREVENTIVE HEALTH SERVICES
Annual checkups, vaccinations (flu, COVID-19), cholesterol tests, and cancer screenings (mammograms, colonoscopy) are fully covered.

4. MENTAL HEALTH SUPPORT
Up to 12 therapy or counseling sessions are covered annually. Includes both in-person and telehealth options. Providers must be in-network.

5. NUTRITION & WEIGHT LOSS
Covers up to 3 dietician consultations per year and discounts on eligible apps and programs (Noom, MyFitnessPal Premium).

6. WELLNESS COACHING
One-on-one wellness coaching (up to 4 sessions/year) is available on request. Topics include stress, sleep, mindfulness, and goal setting.
